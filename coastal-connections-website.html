Our Story

Founded by industry veteran Dean Schoonover, Coastal Connections represents the culmination of decades spent building authentic relationships within the surf, skate, and action sports communities. Our deep understanding of both brand needs and retailer challenges allows us to create partnerships that drive mutual success.

We're not just distributors – we're active participants in the culture we serve. This insider perspective enables us to curate brand portfolios that resonate with core customers while providing retailers with the support they need to thrive in competitive markets.

🏆 Hall of Fame Recognition
