Meet Dean Schoonover

Three decades of industry experience and authentic relationships built on trust and mutual success.

Industry Leadership & Experience

Dean founded Coastal Connections with a simple philosophy: great relationships create great business. With over 30 years in the action sports and retail industry, Dean understands both the challenges retailers face and what brands need to succeed.

This deep industry knowledge allows us to create partnerships that work for everyone. We know which products perform in different markets, understand seasonal trends, and can help you make informed decisions about inventory and merchandising.

Our approach combines old-school relationship building with modern business practices. We believe in personal service, honest communication, and building partnerships that last for years.
