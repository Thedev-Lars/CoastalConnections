About Dean Schoonover

Four decades of building relationships and driving success

With nearly 40 years in the industry, Dean Schoonover's influence in surf culture spans from Sandy Hook to Cape May and beyond. Dean has built an unmatched network of relationships with retailers and brands alike.

Starting with Xcel Wetsuits on the North Shore of Hawaii, Dean has grown to represent some of the most prestigious brands in surf and lifestyle. His approach combines intimate product knowledge, strategic thinking, and the ability to connect the right brands with the right retailers.

Dean's contributions extend beyond sales - he co-designed the Brave New World logo, helped develop Channel Islands' Ave Joe surfboard model for East Coast conditions, and has sponsored countless surfers throughout his career.

"Dean Schoonover has been integral in our success by representing and informing our board designs through the years. His surfing ability and admirable disposition have been an invaluable asset to both CI and NJ surf culture."

- Britt Merrick, Channel Islands Surfboards

"From the very start Dean impressed me with his work ethic, passion for surfing, products and his surfing skills. Over the years he continues to represent XCEL at its highest level and contributes to Xcel's success and surfing's success in N.J."
