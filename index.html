About Coastal Connections

Coastal Connections connects quality brands with retailers who understand their markets. The company specializes in straightforward wholesale partnerships built on mutual success and decades of industry relationships.

Meet Dean Schoonover

Dean Schoonover has dedicated 40 years to the surf industry, evolving from East Coast surfer to one of the most respected sales representatives in the business. His career encompasses representing major international brands, contributing to product development, and building lasting relationships with retailers ranging from core surf shops to major retail chains.

In June 2025, Dean will be inducted into the New Jersey Surfing Hall of Fame, recognizing not only his competitive surfing achievements—including ESA championships and the Belmar Pro—but more importantly, his decades of supporting the surf community through athlete sponsorships and retail partnerships.

Beyond surfing, Dean demonstrated community leadership through nine years of service on the Board of Education, including seven years as President. He currently serves as Secretary of the New Jersey Surfing Hall of Fame.

When retailers partner with Coastal Connections, they work directly with Dean Schoonover. His intimate knowledge of each brand's operations, combined with established relationships at the corporate level, ensures efficient problem-solving and responsive service. Full corporate support teams complement Dean's personal attention.

Dean has built his reputation on accessibility, product knowledge, and understanding regional market dynamics since 1985.

Notable contribution: Dean co-designed the Brave New World logo that remains in use today.
